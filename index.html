Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aetheris (AETH)?

Aetheris is a deflationary ERC-20 token built on the Ethereum blockchain. It features automatic token burns and liquidity addition mechanisms designed to reward long-term holders.

How does the token burn mechanism work?

2% of every sell transaction is automatically and permanently removed from circulation. This reduces the total supply over time, which may contribute to scarcity and potential value appreciation for holders.

What is the maximum wallet size?

The maximum amount any single wallet can hold is limited to 1.5% of the total supply (150,000,000 AETH). This helps prevent large dumps and protects smaller holders.

Is the smart contract ownership renounced?

Ownership has not been renounced yet. The team plans to renounce ownership after liquidity is fully secured and the project reaches a stable phase. Updates will be communicated through official channels.

Where can I purchase AETH tokens?

AETH is available for trading on Uniswap V2. You can use the "BUY AETH" button on this website or add the contract address manually to your wallet.
